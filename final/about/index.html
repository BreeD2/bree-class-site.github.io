About Crystal&Bites

2824 E Broad is a fully leased historic mixed use property with a prominent corner location in Richmond's oldest neighborhood, Church Hill. Built in 1914, this building was renovated in 2010 with new roofs, HVAC, electrical, plumbing, bathrooms, kitchens, windows, walls, and more.

The three (3) renovated apartments, located at both the rear ground floor of the building and on the 2nd floor, offer hardwood floors, granite countertops and stainless steel appliances in the kitchens, new windows throughout, and washer/dryer in each unit. Crystal&Bites is a place with many food selection for anyone of any age to enjoy. With the different food choices, what are you waiting for come and grab some food to go or come eat inside for casual dining.
